1 December 2019, Hubei,China

The first case of Covid-19 appears

The virus is thought to be natural and have an animal origin,speculated a bat, through spillover infection. According to the reports it all started from a local market in Hubei province in Wuhan China

A pneumonia of unknown cause detected in Wuhan, China was first reported to the WHO Country Office in China on 31 December 2019.

The outbreak was declared a Public Health Emergency of International Concern on 30 January 2020.
